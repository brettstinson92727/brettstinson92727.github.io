Project information

  • Category: Shopify Website
  • Client: Thought and Mortar Ltd (Bristol, United Kingdom)
  • Project date: 10 July, 2022
  • Project URL: https://www.evesleep.co.uk

An e-commerce website that sells premium sleep products

Eve Sleep is an e-commerce website that sells premium sleep products, particularly focusing on mattresses. It is known for its award-winning mattress designs that promote comfortable and restorative sleep. The website features a range of sleep solutions, including mattresses with contour zones for better body alignment, pillows, bed frames, and other bedding accessories.

The company aims to deliver high-quality sleep products directly to consumers, making the process of purchasing mattresses and other sleep essentials seamless and convenient online. With a focus on enhancing sleep quality, Eve Sleep appeals to customers who value both comfort and wellness in their sleep routine.

I have been involved in redevelopment of the Eve Sleep website using the Shopify Dawn theme and utilizing the latest Shopify 2.0 features.

My responsibilities

- Customized the Shopify Dawn theme to align with the brand’s aesthetics and improve user experience.

  • Adjusted colors to align with the brand's identity, ensuring a cohesive look throughout the site.

  • Selected and implemented fonts that reflect the brand’s voice, including headings, body text, and buttons.

  • Chose and optimized images that resonate with the target audience, enhancing the visual appeal.

  • Created or modified sections on the homepage, such as hero banners, featured products, and promotional blocks, to highlight key offerings.

  • Organized product collections with custom layouts, grid settings, and filtering options to improve user navigation.

  • Added or modified Shopify sections (using Liquid and JSON) to allow for dynamic content placement, enabling non-technical users to easily update content.

  • Created unique sections for testimonials, blog posts, or FAQs to enhance engagement.

  • Designed a user-friendly navigation menu, including dropdowns, mega menus, and breadcrumb trails to improve site usability.

  • Implemented quick links to key pages (e.g., contact, about, shipping policy) in the footer for easier access.

  • Ensured that all elements are mobile-friendly and that the design adapts seamlessly across various screen sizes and devices.

  • Used media queries and flexible layouts to enhance the mobile experience.

  • Added animations, hover effects, and other interactive features to engage users and improve the overall experience.

  • Implemented calls to action (CTAs) strategically throughout the site to guide users toward desired actions.

  • Structured HTML elements for optimal SEO performance, ensuring proper heading hierarchy and image alt tags are included.

  • Ensured that URLs and metadata align with SEO best practices.

- Integrated Shopify 2.0 features, such as sections and blocks, to enhance the flexibility and functionality of the site.

  • Developed new custom sections tailored to the specific needs of the Eve Sleep brand, such as Hero Section, Featured Products Section, Testimonials Section.

  • Implemented dynamic blocks within sections, enabling content changes on the fly. For example, added or rearranged product blocks within a featured collection without affecting other site elements. Also, customized text blocks for promotions or seasonal offers easily.

  • Created user-friendly settings for each section, allowing non-technical users to update content, adjust images, and modify layouts through the Shopify admin interface.

  • Ensured that section settings included options for background colors, image uploads, and text input, making it easy for the client to personalize their site.

- Optimized product pages with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and customer reviews.

- Conducted performance testing and optimization, focusing on loading times and overall site speed.

- Utilized tools like Google PageSpeed Insights to identify and resolve performance bottlenecks.
